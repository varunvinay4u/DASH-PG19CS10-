Lesson 8: Sounds Lesson


Introduction

In this lesson, we will explore different sounds and learn how to identify them. We will also learn about pronunciation and how to correctly say different words. Also we will practice making our own sounds and matching them to the sounds we hear.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify different sounds
  • Match sounds to corresponding objects or actions
  • Create and produce sounds
  • Learn how to correctly pronounce words
  • Improve overall communication skills
