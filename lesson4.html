Lesson 4: Basic Arithmetics

In this lesson, students will learn and practice important counting and basic math skills. They will begin by reviewing counting numbers, and then move on to addition and subtraction.Students will learn basic math facts, such as addition and subtraction facts up to 10, and multiplication tables up to 5 or 10.

They will also learn about place value, which is the value of a digit based on its position in a number, as well as telling time and counting money.

By the end of this lesson, students will have a solid foundation in counting and basic math, which will serve them well as they continue to build their math skills.
