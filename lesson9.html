Lesson 9: Communication

Welcome to Lesson 9 on Communication and Language. In this lesson, we will explore how to communicate effectively in everyday situations. Communication is an essential life skill that helps us express ourselves, build relationships, and get our needs met. For autistic individuals, communication can be challenging, but with practice and support, it can be improved.

Examples of Communication

Here are some real-world examples of communication:

  • Having a conversation with a friend
  • Ordering food at a restaurant
  • Asking for help when you need it
  • Explaining something to someone
  • Writing an email or text message
  • Making a phone call
