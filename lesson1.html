Lesson 1: Introduction to Colors

Welcome to our first lesson about colors! In this lesson, we'll learn the names of different colors and practice identifying them.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

Identify the names of different colors

Recognize colors in everyday objects

Create simple artworks using different colors

What are colors?

Colors are all around us and they help us make sense of the world. We use colors to identify objects, communicate ideas and emotions, and express ourselves through art.

Let's start by learning the names of some basic colors:

Red

Orange

Yellow

Green

Blue

Purple

There are many other colors, but these are some of the most common ones.

Primary colors

The primary colors are red, blue, and yellow. These colors cannot be made by mixing other colors together.

Secondary colors

The secondary colors are orange, green, and purple. These colors are made by mixing two primary colors together.

Color Identification

Now that we know the names of some colors, let's practice identifying them. Look around you and find objects that are different colors.

Can you name the colors of these objects?

For example:

The sky is blue

The sun is yellow

A leaf is green

An apple is red
