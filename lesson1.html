Lesson 1: Introduction to Colors

In this lesson, we will learn about the basic colors.

What are Colors?

Colors are what we see all around us! The sky is blue, the grass is green, and the sun is yellow. There are many different colors like red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, brown, and black.

Colors can also help us to identify things. For example, when you see a stop sign, you know that it is red and that you should stop. Or when you see a banana, you know that it is yellow and that it is ripe and ready to eat!

How can we learn about Colors?

We can learn about colors by looking at things around us and identifying their colors. You can point to different things and ask what color they are. You can also use coloring books to learn about colors by coloring different pictures.

Today, we are going to learn about some different colors and what they look like!

Primary colors

The primary colors are red, blue, and yellow. These colors cannot be made by mixing other colors together.

Secondary colors

The secondary colors are orange, green, and purple. These colors are made by mixing two primary colors together.

Let's Learn about Some Colors!

Red

Red is a bright and bold color. It is the color of fire trucks and stop signs. It is also the color of apples and cherries.

Blue

Blue is a calming color. It is the color of the sky and the ocean. It is also the color of blueberries and blue jays.

Yellow

Yellow is a bright and cheerful color. It is the color of the sun and sunflowers. It is also the color of bananas and lemons.

Green

Green is a fresh and natural color. It is the color of grass and trees. It is also the color of cucumbers and green beans.
