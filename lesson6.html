Shapes

Today we will learn about different shapes and their properties. Shapes are all around us and understanding them is important for many different activities.

Circle

A circle is a shape with all points at the same distance from its center. It is a two-dimensional shape and has no angles.

Square

A square is a four-sided shape with all sides equal and all angles right angles. It is also a two-dimensional shape.
