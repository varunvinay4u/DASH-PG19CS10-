Lesson 3: Counting Numbers

In this lesson, we will learn about counting numbers.

What are numbers?

Numbers are symbols that we use to represent quantities. We can use numbers to count things, to measure things, and to do many other things.

Counting numbers

The counting numbers are the numbers we use to count things. They are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and so on.

Number recognition

It is important to be able to recognize numbers, so that we can use them to do math and other things. In this lesson, we will practice recognizing numbers.
