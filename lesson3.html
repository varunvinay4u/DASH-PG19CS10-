Lesson 3: Counting and Basic Math

In this lesson, students will learn and practice important counting and basic math skills. They will begin by reviewing counting numbers, and then move on to addition and subtraction.

Students will learn basic math facts, such as addition and subtraction facts up to 10, and multiplication tables up to 5 or 10.

They will also learn about place value, which is the value of a digit based on its position in a number, as well as telling time and counting money.
